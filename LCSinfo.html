Creature Anthology Submission Guidelines

-Due September 30th

-All Submissions must incorporate the theme in some way

-6 Pages or less

-The book will be printed in color so all submissions must incorporate color in some way

-Files must be 300 DPI, CMYK Color, 7in x 10.5 in. Download template Here LCS Comic Template

-Content must be all ages appropriate (we sell these at schools)

-All stories must be self contained (i.e. no “see the ending on my website”)

-We are also accepting Cover submission (same template as interior pages)

-Submit by filling out this online form and attaching files at the bottom Anthology Submission Form
